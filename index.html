Welcome to EyeSpy!

Eye Spy is an image-based scavenger hunt game. A random image from Flickr is chosen and evaluated by an AI called Clarafai.

Clues are given to you based on the information given from the AI. Upload an image based off the clues and see how similar your image was to the original one!

Enter player name here to begin the game.

Clues are given about the random photo pulled from Flickr. The user then has to upload a photo matching those clues.

The two images are compared by using the data given when Clarifai analyzes both images. The scoring is percentage based and is out of 100%.

Compete against your friends and family to see who can score the highest amount of points!
